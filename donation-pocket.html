Every Little Gift Makes a Difference

Welcome to the Donation Pocket! It's a simple way to discover the everyday needs of Juba Rescue Centre. Here you'll find the essential items that help provide food, healthcare, education, shelter and comfort for the children in our care.

Every card tells a story. Click on any card below to flip it over and learn more about that specific need. Whether you're able to donate a bag of rice, a school book, a mosquito net or even a packet of tissues; every contribution matters. Small acts of kindness come together to create lasting change.
