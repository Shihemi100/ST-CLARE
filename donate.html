Why Your Support Matters

Every child deserves more than survival; they deserve safety, education, healthcare, nutritious meals and the opportunity to dream. At Juba Rescue Centre, every act of generosity helps create a loving environment where vulnerable children can heal, learn and build brighter futures.

Whether you choose to donate, volunteer your time, sponsor a project or contribute essential supplies, your kindness becomes part of every child's journey. Together, we can provide hope, stability and opportunities that change lives for generations to come.
