Our Story

Since 2012, Juba Rescue Centre has provided a safe home for vulnerable children, ensuring access to education, healthcare, and emotional support.

Through the support of donors, volunteers, and partners, the centre has transformed lives and continues to build a future filled with dignity and opportunity.
