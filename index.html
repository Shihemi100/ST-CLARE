Our Story

Juba Rescue Centre is a safe and nuturing home dedicated to supporting vulnerable children and young girls in South Sudan.Since 2012, the centre has worked to provide care, protection, education, healthcare and hope to children facing difficult circumstances.

Built on compassion and community support, Juba Rescue Centre continues to create a loving environmnent where every child is valued, protected and given the opportunity to build a brighter future.
