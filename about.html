ABOUT JUBA RESCUE CENTRE |

A place where every child is given safety, love, and a future.

2012 2014 2017 2020 2023 2025

Juba Rescue Centre was founded in 2012 with a simple but powerful goal: to provide a safe and nurturing home for vulnerable children in need of protection, care, and opportunity.

Over the years, the centre has grown into a place of stability and hope, supporting children through education, healthcare, emotional support, and a loving community that values dignity and compassion.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, supportive, and nurturing environment where children can grow, learn, and thrive.

Our Vision

A future where every child has access to care, education, and the opportunity to reach their full potential.
