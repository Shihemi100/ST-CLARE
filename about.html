Juba Rescue Centre was founded in 2012 to provide a safe and nurturing home for vulnerable children.

Over the years, it has grown into a place of stability and hope, supporting children through education, healthcare, emotional support, and a loving community.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, supportive, and nurturing environment where children can grow, learn, and thrive.

Our Vision

A future where every child has access to care, education, and the opportunity to reach their full potential.
